# International Meetings

Real-time bidirectional Chinese-English interpretation integrated with Zoom / Tencent Meeting / Teams — both parties hear their native language.

---

## When to Use

- Participants mix Chinese and English and need bidirectional translation
- You don't want to pay thousands for a human interpreter for a single meeting
- You use mainstream meeting software like Zoom, Tencent Meeting, or Feishu

---

## How It Works

LinkTalk's dual-channel architecture is a natural fit for bidirectional meeting scenarios:

- **Channel A**: I speak Chinese → English output → sent to meeting → they hear English
- **Channel B**: They speak English → Chinese output → sent to headphones → I hear Chinese

The two channels are completely isolated — no interference, no echo, no feedback loops.

Mixed Chinese-English recognition (`zhen` mode) is supported. Expressions like "大家好，今天聊 AI 趋势" (mixing Chinese and English) are accurately recognized and translated.
