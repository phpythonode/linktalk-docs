# Content Export

Quickly convert existing Chinese video content into multi-language dubbed versions and publish to international platforms.

---

## When to Use

- You have Chinese short videos or e-commerce videos and want to publish them on TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram
- You don't want to wait for manual dubbing — you want to record and publish the same day
- You need to cover multiple language markets simultaneously

---

## How It Works

LinkTalk's dual-channel support allows **two streams to be processed simultaneously**, each outputting independently without interference. You can:

- Channel A: process one video's Chinese audio track → output English dubbing
- Channel B: simultaneously process another video → output Japanese dubbing

Two streams in parallel — double the efficiency.

---

## Cost Reference

| Method | Time per video | Language coverage |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| Manual dubbing | 1–3 days/video | 1 language |
| LinkTalk real-time translation | Same as video length | 10+ languages |

> Billed by actual audio duration via the official Volcengine API. New users receive free credits.

---

## Recommended Setup

1. Route the video audio track through a virtual audio device into LinkTalk's "Their Audio Input"
2. Set the output language (e.g., English)
3. Record the translation output as a new audio track and replace the original video audio

For detailed audio routing, see [Virtual Audio Setup](../virtual-audio).
