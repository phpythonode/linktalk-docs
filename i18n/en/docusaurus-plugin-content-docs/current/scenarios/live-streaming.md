# Cross-Border Live Streaming

Stream in Chinese and reach a global audience in their native language — no foreign-language host required.

---

## When to Use

- You want to expand into Southeast Asian, Western, or Japanese live streaming markets
- You don't want to rely on foreign-language hosts (high cost, unreliable)
- You want to engage with overseas audiences in your own voice and style

---

## How It Works

You speak normally in Chinese → LinkTalk recognizes in real time → translates to the target language → outputs in your cloned voice → routed through a virtual audio device into your streaming software.

**First-audio latency: 1.3 seconds** — the audience barely notices any pause. Voice cloning requires no pre-training; it takes effect the moment you go live, preserving your tone and emotion.

## Recommended Setup

1. Microphone → LinkTalk input
2. LinkTalk translation output → Virtual Device A Input
3. OBS / streaming software mic → Virtual Device A Output
4. Go live and speak normally in Chinese
